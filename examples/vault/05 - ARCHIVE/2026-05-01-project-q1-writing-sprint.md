# Q1 Writing Sprint — ARCHIVED

## Outcome (delivered)
Six long-form articles published. Two generated direct inbound inquiries. Hit the target of one article per week for six weeks.

## What worked
- Writing Activator before every draft — eliminated blank-page paralysis
- Permanent notes written immediately after reading, not later
- Output Generator for the synthesis pieces (drew on 3+ months of accumulated notes)

## What did not work
- Daily notes were too long. Need to be capped at 300 words going forward.
- Book notes were summaries, not ideas. Switched to the Key Tension format midway through.
