# Building a Second Brain — Tiago Forte

## Core idea
PARA: Projects, Areas, Resources, Archive. The key distinction is between Projects (defined end date) and Areas (ongoing responsibility). Most people collapse these two and end up with orphaned notes after projects close.

## What stuck
The CODE framework — Capture, Organize, Distill, Express — maps directly to how vault processing should work. Distill is the underrated step: most people capture and organize but never distill the most important idea out of a note.

## Key Tension
Forte's system is primarily a project management tool dressed as a knowledge system. The PARA method tells you where to file things, but not how to connect them or what to do with them once projects complete. [[Permanent Notes Require Atomic Ideas Not Filing Locations]] — a connected permanent note in Resources outlives any project.
