## What Makes a Note Useful For Me
Useful: captures that name a tension or contradiction, captures that connect to an active article topic, anything that challenges an assumption I currently hold
Not useful: raw quotes with no rewriting, notes with no connection to active writing topics, meeting summaries without extracted decisions or actions

## Processing Standards
A note is ready to file when:
- It is written in my own words, not copied from a source
- It connects to at least one existing note
- It could contribute to at least one active project or writing topic
- Its title describes the idea, not the source

A note should be archived when:
- The project or decision it relates to is complete
- The information is outdated or I have written a better version
- It has not been accessed in 6 months and connects to nothing currently active

## Output Goals
- One long-form article per week on knowledge management or local AI
- A synthesized "vault health" review at the end of each quarter showing how many notes actually contributed to published output

## Voice and style
Direct. Specific over general. Tension-forward (lead with what is non-obvious). No preamble. Conclusions before evidence where possible.
